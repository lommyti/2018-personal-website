CollabLab

My work as an undergraduate research assistant.

During my time as an intern at Superfly in Fall 2017, I was given the unique opportunity to pursue a personal project of my choosing. After spending a few weeks learning the ropes, I decided that I wanted to improve the process through which the company layed out websites for music festivals like Outside Lands and Lost Lake, making UX and mobile-friendliness a priority.

To do this, I did an extensive competitor analysis and researched UX conventions to suggest improvements that could be made to current site layouts. This project quickly ballooned into a collaboration with the Design team to evaluate company internal web design processes and practices, emphasize mobile-first development, and to standardize user experience across company websites. The content of this project is protected under an NDA, but more info can be provided upon request!

I prototyped the digital interface for our final project in DSGN 306, User Experience Design; it was an introductory game that would give players a chance to interact with their groupmates and learn about their habits as team members.

As a team, we went through ideation, research, user testing, & prototyping together to create a final product in a 10-week class.

This was a great experience for me to learn , as well as to try my hand at some pixel art.
